Thank You for downloading All Purpose MD5!

All Purpose MD5 is a quick and easy application to calculate MD5 sums.

Long have I looked for a MD5 program that is easy, intuitive and most importantly, fast! I have seen many MD5 programs that do some of this, but many of them are closed source and I was always afraid of spyware or hidden code. Thus, is born All Purpose MD5.
